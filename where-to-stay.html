Where to Stay

We have two room blocks setup, and several options listed below for area hotels. Airbnb is a great option to find an affordable place to stay as well.
 
We live in Greenpoint, Brooklyn about a 20-30 minute car ride from the venue (Green Building); staying pretty much anywhere in Brooklyn or Manhattan will be easy to get back to.

Box Hotel Room Block

In Greenpoint, we have rooms blocked at the Box Hotel. Approximately 30 minutes to the venue. Make reservations by booking online or calling the hotel. Online reservations must provide the event/group name in the "request/remark" section under guest information. When calling to book, identify as members of the group. The block gives 15% off the rack room rate at the hotel until the block is filled up. Event/Group Name: Worrilow/Barrett.

77 Box St, Brooklyn, NY 11222    
(718) 383-3800

Brooklyn Bridge Marriott Room Block

Closer to the venue (15 minutes), we have rooms reserved at the Brooklyn Bridge Marriott at a rate of $239/night. Make reservations via the Passkey link or via (877) 303-0104. Event/Group Name: Worrilow/Barrett.

333 Adams St, Brooklyn, NY 11201    
(718) 246-7000

Additional Accommodations

Below, find some additional recommendations for area hotels. Prices are estimates only. Hipmunk is a great site to find cheap hotel rates in the area.

Airbnb

Airbnb is a great option for finding reasonably priced accommodations for your stay. When looking at locations, we recommend staying in Brooklyn or Manhattan. If staying in Brooklyn, look for locations that are close to the G or F subway lines, and are not too far from the southern/eastern borders of Prospect Park. If you have a question about whether an Airbnb location is conveniently located or safe, feel free to contact us.

Greenpoint / Williamsburg

Franklin Guesthouse

214 Franklin St, Brooklyn, NY 11222    
(718) 383-3900

Prices start high at $379/night but decrease to $250 if you stay 3 nights, and the website will email you a 10% discount code just for signing up. No parking provided, although there is street parking in the area. Just a few blocks away from the G train. Approximately 30 minutes by cab/lyft to the venue.
