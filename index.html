Join us in beautiful Gowanus, Brooklyn on April 13, 2018!!

The Green Building

Details

Doors at 6:00 PM / Ceremony starts promptly at 6:30 PM.

Cocktail Hour will start immediately after the ceremony. We’ll have a buffet dinner starting at 7:30 PM. After we feast, we’ll crank up the tunes and have a rad time & dance our hearts out (until around 1:00 AM).

Unfortunately Meredith nixed the “Time Traveller Formal” & “Wizard Cloaks-Only” dress-code ideas, so we are going with “Dressy Casual.”

Uh … what does that mean? Glad you asked! We’d suggest dresses, slacks, jackets (if you want ‘em), and shoes you can cut a rug in. If you wanna get glammed up or extra fancy, you do you! Come looking sharp and ready for the photobooth!

Guests are on their own for transportation, but there are plenty of options.

Sorry folks, no rugrats. Let us know if you need help making local arrangements for your little monsters!

After Party

286 3rd Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11215
